According to some sources, an average Filipino can spend tens of thousands of pesos yearly on healthcare. This amount might be manageable for some of us, but some people making less than average income, as well as those who are unemployed, cannot afford this. People get many illnesses, communicable diseases, and injuries - some of these could even spread to others or hinder the person in question's daily life. Because of the above reasons, I believe healthcare should be a necessity and not a luxury.
